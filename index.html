About

I am Albert Einstein, a theoretical physicist best known for developing the theory of relativity and the famous equation E = mc². My work also includes contributions to quantum mechanics and the photoelectric effect, for which I received the Nobel Prize in 1921. Throughout my life, I’ve been dedicated to advancing science and advocating for peace and human rights.
